## Rank Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Valorant Rank Boost work?">
    A professional booster plays on your account to reach your selected rank or division. Once the boost is completed, the achieved rank remains permanently on your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is my RR gain important?">
    Your RR gain reflects your hidden MMR. Accounts with low RR gains require more games to rank up. For this reason, pricing is adjusted fairly based on your current RR gain to match the actual workload.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does my Rank Boost start?">
    Most orders start within 15–30 minutes after payment, depending on booster availability. You can track progress directly in your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play while the boost is in progress?">
    Yes, but coordination is important. Please communicate with your booster and never log in at the same time to avoid interruptions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is boosting safe?">
    We use professional boosters, VPN location matching, and manual play only. While all boosting carries some risk under Riot policies, we take maximum precautions to keep your account secure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Win Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Valorant Win Boost work?">
    You purchase a specific number of ranked wins. Our booster plays until the exact number of wins you ordered has been completed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if a game is lost?">
    Lost games are compensated. If a game is lost, the booster will win an additional game so you still receive the full amount of purchased wins.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if a draw is played?">
    Draws are ignored and do not count as wins or losses. They have no negative impact on your order.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does my Win Boost start?">
    Win Boost orders usually begin within 15–30 minutes after purchase, depending on booster availability.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Normal Game Boost

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Normal Game Boost work?">
    A booster plays normal (unrated) games on your account. This service is typically used to level up accounts, complete missions, or improve hidden MMR.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a win guarantee in normal games?">
    No. Since this is an unrated normal game service, we do not offer a specific win guarantee. The focus is on account progress, experience gain, and objective completion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does my Normal Boost start?">
    Orders usually begin shortly after payment, depending on booster availability. You can track the progress in your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play while the boost is active?">
    Yes, but please coordinate with your booster and avoid logging in at the same time to prevent interruptions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
