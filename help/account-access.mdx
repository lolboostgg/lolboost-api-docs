# Account Access for Solo Boosting

LoLBoost offers two ways to play most boosting services:

* **Solo Boosting**: a professional booster logs into your account and plays on your behalf.
* **Duo Queue / Duo Pass**: you stay on your own account and play together with the booster.

Account access only applies to **Solo** orders. If you choose a Duo option, you never share your login.

## If you ordered Solo Boosting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your login details in the order chat">
    Provide your account credentials through the order chat on your order page, never by email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA where supported">
    Two-factor authentication adds a layer of protection. Coordinate with your booster through the order chat if a code is needed to log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Don't log in while the boost is active">
    Logging into your account at the same time as your booster can interrupt the order. Always check the order chat first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Boosting carries some risk of action from the game publisher regardless of precautions taken. See our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service) for details on bans, suspensions, and resets.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Want to avoid sharing your account at all? Choose a **Duo** option on services where it's available, you keep full control of your account the entire time.
</Tip>
