# How to Buy

1. Choose an offer that matches your region, server, rank, or item needs.
2. Review the description, delivery information, and seller details.
3. Complete checkout on LoLBoost.
4. Follow the delivery instructions shown after purchase.
5. Contact support if anything is unclear.

<Info>Always read the offer description before buying, especially for account credentials, delivery time, and region restrictions.</Info>
