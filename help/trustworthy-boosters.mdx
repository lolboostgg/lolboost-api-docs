# How to Choose the Right Boost

LoLBoost works with experienced players across League of Legends, Valorant, and TFT. A few quick checks before you order help make sure you get exactly what you expect.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the service matches your goal">
    Rank Boosting, Win Boosting, Placement Boosting, Duo Pass, and Coaching all solve different problems. Check [What you can do on LoLBoost](/help/what-you-can-do) if you're not sure which one fits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the order options carefully">
    Before checkout, confirm:
    * Your current and target rank or division
    * Server and region
    * Solo (booster plays on your account) vs Duo (you play together)
    * Any extras: preferred champions, VPN, specific booster
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep communication on LoLBoost">
    Use the order chat for everything related to your boost: scheduling, progress updates, and questions. Avoid moving conversations to personal Discord, WhatsApp, or Telegram, that's where most problems start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use support if anything feels off">
    Contact support if your booster is unresponsive for a prolonged period, asks to take payment off-platform, or anything about your order doesn't match what you purchased.
  </Step>
</Steps>
