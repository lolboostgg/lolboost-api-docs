# What you can do on LoLBoost

LoLBoost provides professional boosting, coaching, and account services for **League of Legends**, **Valorant**, and **Teamfight Tactics**. You choose the service and options that fit your goal, LoLBoost handles secure checkout, and an experienced booster or coach delivers your order with live progress in your dashboard.

***

## What can you order?

**Service overview**

| Service | What you get |
| --- | --- |
| **Rank / Win / Placement Boosting** | A booster reaches your target rank, secures wins, or improves your placement results |
| **Duo Pass / Duo Queue** | Play live with a high-elo booster on your own account |
| **Coaching** | Live Co-Pilot coaching or VOD review from a pro |
| **Level & Mastery Boosting** *(LoL)* | Fast account leveling or champion mastery progress |
| **Clash & Arena Boosting** *(LoL)* | Boosted results for Clash tournaments and Arena mode |
| **Pro Games** *(LoL)* | Duo queue with a top-tier player, on your own account |
| **Accounts** | Premium and ready-to-play League of Legends and Valorant accounts |

## How to buy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a game and service">
    Choose League of Legends, Valorant, or TFT, then the service that fits your goal (Rank, Win, Placement, Duo Pass, Coaching, and more).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your order">
    Set your current and target rank, server, and any extras like Duo, a preferred booster, or specific champions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checkout">
    Pay securely and get a confirmation with your order details. See [payment methods](/help/payment-methods).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your boost">
    Follow live progress from your dashboard and message your booster directly. See [Tracking Your Boost](/help/boost-progress).

</Steps>
