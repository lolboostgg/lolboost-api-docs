# What you can do on LoLBoost

LoLBoost is a marketplace for League of Legends, Valorant, and Teamfight Tactics services: rank boosting, placement matches, coaching, and ready-to-play accounts. Buyers choose an offer that fits their goal, LoLBoost handles checkout and payment, and a verified seller delivers the order.

Verified sellers list and manage their own offers and are responsible for delivering them accurately and on time, in line with the LoLBoost [seller guide](/partner-center/seller-guide). LoLBoost runs the systems behind the scenes: secure checkout, order tracking, and support if anything goes wrong.

***

## For buyers

### How to buy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a service">
    Choose between boosting (solo, duo, or placements), coaching, or a premium account, for League of Legends, Valorant, or TFT.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your order">
    Set your current and target rank, region, and any extras like duo queue or a preferred booster.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checkout">
    Pay securely and get a confirmation with your order details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track delivery">
    Follow progress from your LoLBoost order page and message your seller directly if you have questions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

See [Order problem](/help/order-problem) if something doesn't go as expected after checkout.

### Find the right offer

Use search and filters to narrow offers by game, rank range, region, and delivery method (solo or duo). Listing pages show the seller's rating, completion rate, and exactly what's included, so you can compare offers before buying.
