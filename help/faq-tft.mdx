## Rank Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How Does TFT Rank Boost Work?">
    TFT rank boosting involves a professional booster playing on your account to help you reach your desired rank. Once the boost is complete, you will retain the achieved rank on your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long until my TFT Boosting starts?">
    Boosting typically begins within 15-30 minutes after your order is placed, depending on booster availability. You can check updates or communicate with your booster through your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I Play with the Booster?">
    Standard TFT Ranked is solo-only. If you want to play with a booster, choose a Double Up / Duo option (if available) during the order process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I still play TFT while my Rank Boost is in progress?">
    Absolutely, but communication with your booster is essential. Use the dashboard to communicate and ensure you don't log into your account simultaneously while the booster is playing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I Request The Same Booster For A Future Purchase?">
    Yes, you can! Simply choose the Booster in the Checkout or contact our support team, and we will do our best to assign the same booster for your future orders.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get banned for TFT rank boosting?">
    While boosting carries a risk of account suspension due to Riot Games' policies, we take extensive precautions such as using VPNs to match your location and minimizing suspicious activity to keep your account safe.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Win Boosting (Top 4)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What Is A Top 4 Win Boost?">
    A Top 4 Win Boost guarantees a specific number of Top 4 finishes in TFT, which count as “wins” for the boost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I continue playing TFT while my Win Boost is in progress?">
    Yes, you can continue playing. However, please coordinate with your booster via the customer dashboard to avoid logging in at the same time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if the Booster loses a game?">
    If the booster finishes outside Top 4 during a Win Boost, additional games will be played to secure the purchased number of Top 4 wins. For example, if you buy 5 wins and the booster finishes 5th–8th in 3 games, service continues until you have 5 total Top 4 finishes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play with the booster in Duo (Double Up) Boosting?">
    Standard TFT Ranked is solo-only. If you want to play with a booster, choose a Double Up / Duo option (if available) in the extra options before clicking “Buy Boost Now”.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my MMR is higher?">
    TFT uses hidden MMR which affects LP gains and match difficulty. Please select the average lobby level of your recent games rather than only your displayed rank. If your MMR is much higher than your rank, choosing the higher option ensures correct expectations and best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Placement Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a Placement Boosting service?">
    A professional TFT boosting service that secures strong placement results in your placement matches, giving you a better start to the season.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play Duo with the booster?">
    Standard TFT Ranked placements are solo-only. If you want to play together, choose a Double Up / Duo option (if available) in order customization before clicking “Buy Boost Now”.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many wins are guaranteed?">
    If your final placement lands one full tier/division higher than expected due to your MMR, the boost is considered completed regardless of exact Top 4 count.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I buy if I already played some placements?">
    Yes. Choose your last season’s rank/MMR level and set the number of remaining placements with the slider above.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I was ranked before but not last season?">
    Select the rank from your last ranked season. Your hidden TFT MMR persists, so you’ll face similarly skilled lobbies even if you skipped a season.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if the guaranteed amount isn’t reached?">
    We keep playing until the guarantee is met. If the agreed Top 4 rate isn’t achieved, extra Top 4 wins are added for games outside Top 4 beyond the guarantee to fulfill the promise.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Double Up Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is TFT Double Up Boosting?">
    TFT Double Up is a 2v2v2v2 mode where you queue as a duo and share resources with your partner. Double Up Boosting helps you climb your Double Up rank faster with a professional partner.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as a win in Double Up?">
    In Double Up, a “win” for Win Boosting is counted when your team finishes in the top 2 (1st or 2nd). Finishing 3rd or 4th counts as a loss.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if the booster loses a game?">
    If your team finishes 3rd or 4th, it counts as a loss. The booster will continue playing additional games until you receive the total number of wins you purchased (top 2 finishes).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play with the booster (Duo / Play-With)?">
    Yes—if you choose a “Play-With” Double Up option, you will queue together with the booster as your partner. This is the safest and most interactive method because you stay on your own account the whole time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I still play while my Double Up boost is in progress?">
    If it is a Solo Boost (booster plays on your account), please do not play at the same time to avoid login conflicts. Coordinate through your customer dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if we rank up and still have wins remaining?">
    If you rank up faster than expected during Double Up Win Boosting, the remaining wins may be adjusted depending on the selected package and the achieved milestone.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
