# Contacting Your Booster or Coach

Every order on LoLBoost includes a direct order chat with your assigned booster or coach.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your order chat">
    Go to **My Orders** in your dashboard, open the order in question, and you'll land on the page with your order chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use it for everything order-related">
    Scheduling, progress updates, account access for Solo boosts, preferred champions or roles, anything about your order belongs in the order chat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Booster availability

Boosters occasionally step away (breaks, other orders, time zones). If your booster isn't responding right away, give it a short window before assuming something is wrong, queue times and availability vary by rank and server.

<Card title="If your booster doesn't respond" icon="triangle-exclamation">
  If your booster is unresponsive in the order chat for a prolonged period, [open a support ticket](/help/order-problem) and our team will step in.
</Card>

<Card title="Keep communication on LoLBoost" icon="shield">
  Keep all order-related communication on LoLBoost so support can step in if something goes wrong. We can't fully assist with issues that happened entirely off-platform.
</Card>
