# LoLBoost Discord Community

Join our official Discord to follow updates, events, and community discussions:

👉 [lolboost.gg/discord](https://lolboost.gg/discord)

***

## Why join?

* **Giveaways & events**: get updates on giveaways, events, and community activities
* **Announcements**: see news about new features, services, and important changes
* **Community chat**: talk with other players, share feedback, and discuss your games

***

## Security Notice: Watch for Impersonators

Be cautious of people pretending to be LoLBoost staff on Discord.

* Staff will never send you a direct message first
* Staff will never send you a friend request
* If someone contacts you claiming to be staff, treat it as suspicious, don't click unknown links or download any files
* If unsure, [open a support ticket](/help/order-problem) and include screenshots

<Warning>
  Keep all important communication and payments within official LoLBoost channels: the website order chat or the official Discord. Never pay or share account credentials with someone contacting you off-platform.
</Warning>
