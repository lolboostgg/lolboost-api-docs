# Get started with LoLBoost

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account or log in">
    Sign up on [lolboost.gg](https://lolboost.gg) with your email, or use a social login if available. You'll need a verified account to place orders and track your boost.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Click your profile icon in the top right corner to access your dashboard once you're logged in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Your dashboard is where everything about your order lives:
    * **My Orders**: view and manage current and past boosts
    * **Order Chat**: message your assigned booster or coach directly
    * **Wallet**: check your store credit balance
    * **Settings**: manage your account, password, and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your first order">
    Pick your game (League of Legends, Valorant, or TFT) and your service (Rank, Win, Placement, Duo Pass, Coaching, and more), customize the options, and check out.
  </Step>
</Steps>
