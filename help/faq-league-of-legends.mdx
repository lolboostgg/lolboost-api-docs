## Rank Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How Does Rank Boosting Work?">

  <Accordion title="Can I Play with the Booster?">

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I still play the game while my LoL Rank Boost is in progress?">
    Absolutely, but communication with your booster is essential. Use the dashboard to communicate and ensure you don't log into your account simultaneously while the booster is playing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I Request The Same Booster For A Future Purchase?">
    Yes, you can! Simply choose the Booster in the Checkout or contact our support team, and we will do our best to assign the same booster for your future orders.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get banned for LoL division boosting?">
    While boosting carries a risk of account suspension due to Riot Games' policies, we take extensive precautions such as using VPNs to match your location and minimizing suspicious activity to keep your account safe.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Win Boosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What Is A Net Win Boost?">
    A Net Win Boost guarantees a specific number of net victories, meaning the difference between wins and losses achieved during the boost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I continue playing the game while my LoL Win Boost is in progress?">
    Yes, you can continue playing during your Win Boost. However, please coordinate with your booster via the customer dashboard to avoid logging in at the same time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if the Booster loses a game?">
    If the booster loses a game during a Win Boost, additional games will be played to secure the purchased number of wins. For example, if you buy 5 wins and the booster loses 3 games, service continues until you have 8 total games (5 wins and 3 losses).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play with the booster in Duo Queue Boosting?">
    Yes. Select the “Duo” option in the extra options before clicking “Buy Boost Now”.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my MMR is higher?">
    Please select the average rank of your recent matches rather than only your displayed rank. For instance, if you are shown as Gold but play at a Platinum MMR, choose Platinum when purchasing a Win Boost. This ensures proper match difficulty and best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
