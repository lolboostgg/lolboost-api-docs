## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why was my payment declined?">
    Declines are usually issued by your card or bank, not LoLBoost. Double-check your card details, available balance, and that your bank allows online gaming purchases. If it keeps failing, try a different payment method or [contact support](/help/order-problem).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is checkout secure?">
    Yes. Payments are processed through encrypted, secure payment providers, LoLBoost does not store your full card details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
