# Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your LoLBoost account through your account settings, or by contacting support.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Sign in to your LoLBoost account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to your account **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request deletion">
    Look for a **Delete Account** option, or [contact support](/help/order-problem) to request deletion if you don't see one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, you lose access to your order history and any remaining store credit.
</Warning>

If you have any open orders, resolve or complete them first, support may not be able to delete an account with active orders.

If you have any questions or concerns before deleting your account, [contact our support team](mailto:support@lolboost.gg).
