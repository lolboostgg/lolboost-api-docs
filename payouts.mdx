# Payouts

Seller payouts are handled inside the Seller Area. Keep your payment details up to date and check pending balances regularly.

<Card title="Request a payout" icon="wallet" href="https://lolboost.gg/seller-area/payout-requests">
  Open Payout Requests in the Seller Area to request a withdrawal.
</Card>

## Payout fees

Standard payout fees:

| Method | Fee |
| --- | --- |
| Bank Transfer | 3% |
| Crypto | 5% |

The payout fee is deducted from the requested payout amount before the payout is sent.
